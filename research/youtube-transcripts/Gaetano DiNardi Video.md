# Gaetano DiNardi Video Research

Video Title:
Future Search for SaaS: A New Era of Growth and Discovery | Gaetano DiNardi

Video Link:
https://youtu.be/wmXperCHU18?si=wLRsSsaXZMhStQmc  

## Key Insights:

* Traditional SEO attribution models are becoming less reliable as buyers increasingly conduct research through AI assistants, video platforms, and other non-traditional discovery channels.
* The "dark SEO funnel" means influence often happens long before measurable website visits or conversions occur.
* Search optimization now extends beyond Google and requires visibility across YouTube, social media platforms, AI search engines, and other discovery surfaces.
* Strong entity associations and brand recognition are becoming more important than individual keyword rankings in AI-driven search environments.
* Product-led, insight-driven content significantly outperforms generic informational content that lacks a unique perspective or connection to the product.

## Why This Matters:

The way B2B buyers discover, evaluate, and purchase software is changing rapidly. Search engines are no longer the sole gateway to information, and traditional attribution models struggle to capture the influence of AI assistants, video content, social platforms, and community discussions. Companies that continue measuring SEO solely through traffic and conversions risk undervaluing their true market influence. To remain competitive, B2B SaaS marketers must embrace a "Search Everywhere" strategy that combines entity building, video content, product expertise, and omnichannel visibility to remain present throughout the buyer journey.

## Top 3 Takeaways:

1. **Optimize for influence, not just clicks** — Buyer decisions are increasingly shaped in channels that traditional attribution models cannot fully track.
2. **Invest in video and entity building** — Strong brand associations and discoverability across platforms are becoming critical ranking factors in AI-powered search.
3. **Create unique, product-led content** — Generic informational content is being commoditized; human expertise, original insights, and product relevance create durable competitive advantages.
